MEDICAL AUTHORIZATION LETTER GUIDELINES

REQUIREMENTS:
- Professional medical language
- Clear articulation of medical necessity
- Specific clinical justification
- Conservative treatment failure documentation
- Objective findings and functional impairment

PAYER RULES:
- United Healthcare: Requires 6+ weeks conservative treatment, objective findings, functional impairment documentation
- Aetna: Emphasizes clinical guidelines compliance, detailed imaging justification
- Cigna: Focus on medical necessity criteria, prior authorization history
- Blue Cross Blue Shield: State-specific requirements, detailed clinical documentation

CLINICAL GUIDELINES:
- Reference appropriate medical specialty guidelines
- Include evidence-based treatment protocols
- Document failed conservative measures
- Specify functional limitations and work impact
- Include relevant imaging findings and clinical correlation

DOCUMENTATION STANDARDS:
- Complete patient demographics
- Accurate diagnosis codes (ICD-10)
- Proper procedure codes (CPT)
- Detailed treatment history
- Objective clinical findings
- Functional assessment
- Work status impact
- Conservative treatment failure documentation

APPEAL SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS:
- Address specific denial reasons
- Provide additional clinical evidence
- Reference payer policy compliance
- Include updated documentation
- Emphasize medical necessity
- Highlight functional impairment
- Document conservative treatment failure
- Include specialist recommendations if applicable
